Vilage's of Harsil valley

Bagori Village is a picturesque and serene village located in the Harsil Valley of Uttarakhand, India. Nestled in the Himalayan region, Bagori is known for its natural beauty, traditional wooden houses, and rich cultural heritage. It is situated near the famous town of Harsil, which is a popular stop for pilgrims traveling to the Gangotri Temple, one of the four sacred sites in the Chota Char Dham pilgrimage
